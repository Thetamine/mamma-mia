Orlando's Best Pizza and Wings Are From the City's Only Premier Family Owned and Operated Pizzeria! Since 1996, Mama Mia's Pizzeria has been hand-crafting Orlando's favorite pizza, calzones, stromboli, wings and so much more. All of our ingredients are individually selected to assure the best and most authentic tasting New York style pizza south of Manhattan!
